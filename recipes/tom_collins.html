Tom Collins

Description

The secret to a Tom Collins is the glass. No, seriously. The tall Collins glass makes this tall frosty gin and lemon drink a pleasure to look at, to watch be made, and to drink.

The clean, flavor is a crowd pleaser for men and women alike, especially at the height of summer.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz gin
  • 1 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar (or Â½ oz simple syrup)
  • Soda water to fill

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients beside the soda water to a shaker and shake vigorously
  2. Pour into a glass of ice
  3. Fill the rest of the glass with soda water
  4. Garnish with a slice of lemon and add a straw
