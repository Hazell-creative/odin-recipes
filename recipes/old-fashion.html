Old Fashion

Description

One of the great classic bourbon cocktails

The Old Fashioned was invented in Louisville, KY. Extremly simple, extremly satisfying

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp sugar syrup or 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1-2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 60ml Scotch whisky or bourbon
  • orange slice
  • soda water (optional)
  • maraschino cherry (optional)

Steps

  1. Pour the simple syrup, water, and bitters into a whiskey glass.
  2. Stir to combine, then place the ice cubes in the glass.
  3. Pour bourbon over the ice
  4. Garnish with the orange slice and maraschino cherry
